# The Kidnapped Prime Minister

## 剧集信息

- 剧名：*Agatha Christie's Poirot*
- 中文：大侦探波洛
- 季集：S02E08
- 英文标题：*The Kidnapped Prime Minister*
- 中文可译：首相绑架案
- 首播：1990-02-25
- 主角：Hercule Poirot
- 雅思目标：政府责任、危机管理、证据判断、国家安全

资料来源显示，这一集是 ITV 版《Agatha Christie's Poirot》第二季第 8 集，改编自阿加莎·克里斯蒂的同名短篇故事。故事发生在重大国际会议前夕，英国首相和秘书在法国被绑架，波洛被紧急召来处理这起高风险案件。

## 一句话剧情

英国首相在重要会议前失踪，事件迅速从个人安全问题上升为国家危机。波洛需要在有限时间内判断：袭击、车辆、证人说法和官方解释中，哪些是真实信息，哪些可能只是误导视线的安排。

## 本集雅思学习重点

这一集适合转化为三类雅思能力：

1. 阅读：练习政治、危机、时间压力类文章的信息定位。
2. 口语：讨论政府责任、领导力、危机处理和公众安全。
3. 写作：围绕 national security, crisis management, public trust 建立观点。
4. 词汇：从 kidnap / urgent 升级到 Band 7+ 的抽象表达。

## 高阶词汇

### national security

含义：国家安全。

搭配：

- a matter of national security：国家安全事项
- protect national security：保护国家安全
- national security concerns：国家安全担忧

例句：

> The kidnapping becomes a matter of national security rather than a private crime.

这起绑架不再只是私人犯罪，而成为国家安全问题。

### crisis management

含义：危机管理。

搭配：

- effective crisis management：有效危机管理
- a crisis management strategy：危机管理策略
- improve crisis management：改善危机管理

例句：

> Effective crisis management requires accurate information and calm decision-making.

有效危机管理需要准确信息和冷静决策。

### diplomatic

含义：外交的；有策略的。

搭配：

- diplomatic negotiations：外交谈判
- a diplomatic crisis：外交危机
- diplomatic consequences：外交后果

例句：

> The incident may have serious diplomatic consequences if it delays the conference.

如果事件延误会议，可能产生严重外交后果。

### credibility

含义：可信度；公信力。

搭配：

- lose credibility：失去公信力
- restore credibility：恢复可信度
- political credibility：政治公信力

例句：

> A government loses credibility when its official explanation cannot be verified.

当官方解释无法被核实时，政府会失去公信力。

### contingency plan

含义：应急预案。

搭配：

- prepare a contingency plan：准备应急预案
- have no contingency plan：没有应急预案
- activate a contingency plan：启动应急预案

例句：

> A political crisis reveals whether leaders have a realistic contingency plan.

政治危机会暴露领导者是否拥有现实可行的应急预案。

### mislead

含义：误导。

搭配：

- mislead the public：误导公众
- deliberately mislead someone：故意误导某人
- misleading evidence：误导性证据

例句：

> Some details may have been arranged to mislead investigators.

某些细节可能是为了误导调查者而安排的。

### verify

含义：核实；证实。

搭配：

- verify official information：核实官方信息
- verify a timeline：核实时间线
- independently verified：被独立核实

例句：

> In a crisis, information must be verified before decisions are made.

在危机中，信息必须先被核实，然后才能作出决策。

### accountability

含义：问责；责任承担。

搭配：

- political accountability：政治问责
- public accountability：公共责任
- strengthen accountability：加强问责

例句：

> Public accountability is essential when government decisions affect national safety.

当政府决策影响国家安全时，公共问责非常重要。

## 雅思阅读训练

### 信息定位

```text
Setting: before an important international conference.
Problem: the Prime Minister disappears after an apparent attack.
Risk: national security, diplomatic delay, public panic.
Evidence: official cars, witness statements, timing, route.
Question: which facts are reliable and which details are misleading?
```

### 同义替换

| 剧情表达 | 雅思阅读替换 |
| --- | --- |
| the Prime Minister is kidnapped | a national leader is abducted |
| an important conference | a high-level diplomatic meeting |
| time is running out | there is severe time pressure |
| the government is worried | officials express serious concern |
| a false clue | misleading evidence |
| check the official story | verify the official account |

### 判断题练习

1. The case is treated as a national issue rather than an ordinary crime.
2. Poirot relies entirely on the first official explanation.
3. The time pressure increases the risk of poor decision-making.

参考思路：

- 第 1 题：True。首相失踪必然上升到国家层面。
- 第 2 题：False。波洛会核实和质疑说法。
- 第 3 题：True。时间压力会影响判断。

## 雅思口语训练

### Part 2：Describe a time when someone had to make a decision under pressure

```text
I would like to describe an episode of Agatha Christie's Poirot called The Kidnapped Prime Minister.
In the story, the British Prime Minister disappears before an important international conference.
The situation is extremely serious because it is connected not only with personal safety but also with national security.
Poirot has to work under severe time pressure, but he remains calm and tries to verify the evidence.
What impressed me is that he does not simply accept the official account. Instead, he examines the timeline and asks whether some details are misleading.
This episode shows that good decision-making under pressure depends on discipline, evidence and clear reasoning.
```

### Part 3：How should governments respond to a crisis?

```text
Governments should respond to a crisis with speed, transparency and accuracy.
Speed is important because delays may worsen the situation, but speed without verification can create misinformation.
Therefore, officials need a clear contingency plan, reliable communication channels and a willingness to correct mistakes.
In my view, public trust depends not on pretending that everything is under control, but on showing accountability and competence.
```

### Part 3：Is it difficult for leaders to make decisions under pressure?

```text
Yes, because pressure reduces the time available for careful analysis.
Leaders may be tempted to choose the most convenient explanation or follow public emotion.
However, responsible leadership requires the ability to separate reliable evidence from misleading information, especially when national interests are involved.
```

## 雅思写作训练

### 可迁移话题

- Governments should be transparent during emergencies.
- Leaders need evidence-based decision-making.
- Public safety sometimes conflicts with public information.
- Crisis management is a key measure of political competence.

### 写作观点 1：危机中速度和准确性同样重要

```text
In a public crisis, rapid action is necessary, but it should not come at the expense of accuracy.
If governments release unverified information, they may create panic, damage credibility and make the crisis harder to control.
For this reason, crisis management should combine quick response with careful verification.
```

### 写作观点 2：透明沟通有助于维护公众信任

```text
Public trust is more likely to be maintained when governments communicate honestly during emergencies.
This does not mean that every detail must be disclosed immediately, especially when national security is involved.
However, leaders should explain what is known, what remains uncertain and what actions are being taken.
```

### Task 2 练习题

```text
In times of national crisis, some people believe governments should control information to avoid public panic.
Others believe the public has the right to know as much as possible.
Discuss both views and give your own opinion.
```

建议立场：

政府可以暂时保留敏感细节，但应尽量透明说明事实、风险和行动；完全封锁信息会削弱公信力。
