# The Million Dollar Bond Robbery

## 剧集信息

- 剧名：*Agatha Christie's Poirot*
- 中文：大侦探波洛
- 季集：S03E03
- 英文标题：*The Million Dollar Bond Robbery*
- 中文可译：百万美元债券失窃案
- 首播：1991-01-13
- 主角：Hercule Poirot, Hastings, Miss Lemon
- 本节目标：从英语逻辑出发，训练“开口说”的句子骨架

资料来源显示，这一集围绕一批价值百万美元的 Liberty Bonds 展开。债券在运往美国途中失窃，负责押运的 Philip Ridgeway 成为嫌疑人。Poirot 需要判断：是押运人监守自盗，还是有人提前设计了一个看似合理的陷阱。

## 一句话剧情

A young banker is suspected of stealing a shipment of valuable bonds, but Poirot suspects that the obvious explanation may be too convenient.

中文意思：

一个年轻银行职员被怀疑偷走了一批贵重债券，但波洛认为这个“显而易见”的解释可能太顺手、太像被安排好的了。

这一句很适合练口语，因为它不是逐字翻译中文，而是典型英语表达：

```text
人物 + is suspected of doing something, but + 另一个人物 + suspects that...
```

你开口时不用先想复杂语法，只要抓住：

```text
Someone is suspected of something, but I think...
```

## 今天先建立一个底层逻辑

中文说话常常喜欢先铺背景：

```text
这集讲的是有一批债券要运到美国，然后中途不见了，所以那个负责押运的人被怀疑了。
```

英语更常见的顺序是先给主干：

```text
The episode is about a robbery.
```

然后一层一层往外加：

```text
The episode is about a robbery involving a shipment of valuable bonds.
```

再加人物和冲突：

```text
The episode is about a robbery involving a shipment of valuable bonds, and a young banker becomes the main suspect.
```

所以哑巴英语最要紧的不是先背高级词，而是养成这个顺序：

```text
先说核心事实 -> 再说补充信息 -> 最后说你的判断
```

## 句子拆解：英语为什么这样说

### 1. The episode is about...

这是最稳的开口句。

不要一上来想“我今天想讲的是……”，直接说：

```text
The episode is about a robbery.
The story is about a missing shipment.
The case is about a young man who is wrongly suspected.
```

为什么这样说？

因为英语句子需要一个很清楚的主干。`The episode is about...` 的底层逻辑是：

```text
我先告诉你这东西属于什么主题。
```

中文可以说“这集吧，就是那个债券被偷了”；英语更喜欢先摆出一个稳定结构：

```text
This episode is about + 名词
```

你真正要练的是把中文脑内一团剧情压缩成一个名词：

| 中文脑内想法 | 英语先压缩成 |
| --- | --- |
| 债券被偷了 | a robbery |
| 有人被怀疑了 | a suspicion |
| 波洛查真相 | an investigation |
| 表面解释不可靠 | a misleading explanation |

### 2. Philip Ridgeway is suspected of stealing the bonds.

这句的重点是 `be suspected of doing`。

中文说：“大家怀疑他偷了债券。”

英语当然也可以说：

```text
People suspect him of stealing the bonds.
```

但侦探剧、新闻、法律语境更常用被动：

```text
He is suspected of stealing the bonds.
```

为什么？

因为这句话真正重要的不是“谁怀疑他”，而是“他处在被怀疑的位置”。英语会把重要信息放到主语位置：

```text
He is suspected...
```

你开口时可以套这个：

```text
He is suspected of lying.
She is suspected of hiding something.
The manager is suspected of planning the theft.
```

注意：`of` 后面接动名词：

```text
suspected of stealing
suspected of lying
suspected of manipulating the evidence
```

### 3. Poirot does not jump to conclusions.

这句是非常好的口语句。

`jump to conclusions` 不是“跳到结论”，而是“过早下结论”。

英语里 `jump` 有一个底层画面：从证据直接跳到结论，中间的推理过程被省略了。所以这句话的逻辑是：

```text
Poirot does not move too quickly from appearance to judgement.
```

你可以这样说自己：

```text
I sometimes jump to conclusions.
I should not jump to conclusions before I know the facts.
```

这比中文式的 `I should not make a judgement too early` 更口语。

### 4. The obvious explanation may be misleading.

这句是波洛式思维的核心。

`obvious` 是“明显的”，但明显不等于真实。`misleading` 是“会把你带错方向的”。

英语这样说，是因为它把抽象判断变成了两个标签：

```text
obvious explanation
misleading explanation
plausible explanation
convenient explanation
```

你只要会替换形容词，就能说很多句：

```text
The explanation sounds plausible.
The explanation is too convenient.
The explanation may be incomplete.
The explanation does not fit the evidence.
```

这就是英语口语的底层玩法：不是每次重新造句，而是固定名词，替换前面的形容词。

## 本集高频表达

### bond

含义：债券。

本集的 `bond` 不是“纽带”，而是金融意义上的债券。`Liberty Bonds` 指美国历史上的自由债券。

口语里不用解释太复杂，可以说：

```text
Bonds are financial documents that represent money or debt.
```

### shipment

含义：一批运输中的货物。

比 `things` 准确很多。

```text
a shipment of bonds
a shipment of jewellery
a valuable shipment
```

### suspect

可以作动词，也可以作名词。

```text
I suspect that he is lying.
He is the main suspect.
```

底层逻辑：

```text
suspect 作动词：我怀疑
suspect 作名词：嫌疑人
```

### keep an eye on

含义：盯着；留意；看管。

这比 `watch` 更自然。

```text
Poirot is asked to keep an eye on the bonds.
```

为什么不是只说 `watch the bonds`？

因为 `keep an eye on` 带有“持续留意、防止出事”的意思，不是简单看一眼。

### clear someone's name

含义：洗清某人的嫌疑。

```text
His fiancee asks Poirot to clear his name.
```

中文说“证明他是清白的”；英语常说“清理他的名字”。英语把“名声/身份”看成一个可以被污名化、也可以被洗清的东西。

### frame someone

含义：陷害某人。

```text
He may have been framed.
```

这个句子很短，但非常有用。侦探剧里如果你说不出长句，就先说：

```text
Maybe he was framed.
```

这就是能开口的句子。

## 哑巴英语开口训练

### 第一层：只说事实

先不要追求完整复述，只说 5 个短句：

```text
The episode is about a robbery.
The bonds are missing.
Philip is suspected.
Poirot investigates the case.
The obvious explanation may be wrong.
```

这五句已经能完成一次基础表达。

### 第二层：加因果

英语里最常用的推进词是 `because`, `so`, `but`。

```text
The bonds are missing, so Philip is suspected.
Philip is suspected because he was responsible for the shipment.
The case looks simple, but Poirot does not jump to conclusions.
```

你开口时优先用这三个词，不要急着用复杂连接词。

### 第三层：加判断

```text
I think the case is interesting because the obvious suspect may not be guilty.
```

这句很适合背下来。结构是：

```text
I think + 观点 + because + 理由
```

你可以替换：

```text
I think Poirot is impressive because he focuses on small details.
I think the story is clever because it plays with our assumptions.
I think the suspect is unlucky because the evidence points to him.
```

## 从中文到英语：不要逐字翻译

### 中文 1

```text
他负责押运债券，所以债券丢了之后大家都怀疑他。
```

不要翻成：

```text
He responsible transport bonds, so everyone doubt him.
```

英语要先确定动词：

```text
He was responsible for transporting the bonds, so everyone suspected him.
```

更自然一点：

```text
He was responsible for the shipment, so he became the main suspect.
```

这里的英语逻辑是：

```text
身份/责任 -> 结果/处境
```

`became the main suspect` 比 `everyone suspected him` 更像侦探剧表达，因为重点落在“他变成了主要嫌疑人”。

### 中文 2

```text
波洛觉得这个解释太明显了，可能是有人故意让大家这样想。
```

可以说：

```text
Poirot thinks the explanation is too obvious, and someone may want people to believe it.
```

更顺一点：

```text
Poirot suspects that the obvious explanation has been deliberately created.
```

底层逻辑：

```text
Poirot suspects that + 一个完整句子
```

这个结构很好用：

```text
Poirot suspects that someone is lying.
Poirot suspects that the evidence has been planted.
Poirot suspects that the theft was planned in advance.
```

### 中文 3

```text
我觉得这集的重点不是债券，而是人们太容易相信表面证据。
```

可以说：

```text
I think the point of the episode is not the bonds, but how easily people trust surface evidence.
```

这个结构非常高级，但其实很好套：

```text
The point is not A, but B.
```

练习：

```text
The point is not the crime, but the logic behind it.
The point is not the suspect, but the way evidence is interpreted.
The point is not money, but trust.
```

## 口语复述模板

先背这个版本，控制在 40 秒左右：

```text
I watched an episode of Agatha Christie's Poirot called The Million Dollar Bond Robbery.
The episode is about a valuable shipment of bonds that goes missing on the way to America.
A young banker is suspected because he was responsible for the shipment.
However, Poirot does not jump to conclusions.
He suspects that the obvious explanation may be misleading.
What I like about the story is that it shows the importance of checking evidence carefully before blaming someone.
```

这段的英语逻辑很清楚：

```text
我看了什么 -> 故事讲什么 -> 谁被怀疑 -> 波洛怎么想 -> 我为什么喜欢
```

你以后讲任何一集都可以用这个顺序。

## 今日重点句式

### 1. A is suspected of B

```text
Philip is suspected of stealing the bonds.
```

替换：

```text
The secretary is suspected of hiding the truth.
The passenger is suspected of helping the thief.
The manager is suspected of manipulating the evidence.
```

### 2. The case looks simple, but...

```text
The case looks simple, but Poirot notices something unusual.
```

替换：

```text
The case looks simple, but the timeline does not make sense.
The case looks simple, but the evidence is too convenient.
The case looks simple, but one detail changes everything.
```

### 3. It turns out that...

含义：结果发现……

```text
It turns out that the first explanation is not reliable.
```

这个句式特别适合复述剧情，因为侦探剧总有反转。

```text
It turns out that he was innocent.
It turns out that the theft was planned earlier.
It turns out that the evidence was misleading.
```

### 4. What I found interesting was...

```text
What I found interesting was Poirot's attitude toward evidence.
```

这比 `I think it is interesting` 更有层次。

底层结构：

```text
What I found interesting was + 名词
```

替换：

```text
What I found interesting was the false accusation.
What I found interesting was the way Poirot questioned the obvious answer.
What I found interesting was the contrast between appearance and truth.
```

## 练习任务

### 开口 1 分钟

不要看中文，按下面提示说：

```text
I watched...
The episode is about...
At first...
However...
What I found interesting was...
```

### 句子替换

用本集内容替换括号：

```text
The episode is about (a robbery).
Philip is suspected of (stealing the bonds).
Poirot suspects that (the obvious explanation is misleading).
The case looks simple, but (one detail does not fit).
What I found interesting was (Poirot's careful reasoning).
```

### 中文转英文

把下面三句说出来，不要求一字不差：

1. 这集讲的是一批债券失窃。
2. 负责押运的人被怀疑偷了债券。
3. 波洛没有急着下结论，因为表面证据可能会误导人。

参考答案：

```text
This episode is about a shipment of bonds that goes missing.
The man responsible for the shipment is suspected of stealing the bonds.
Poirot does not jump to conclusions because surface evidence can be misleading.
```
